EPISODE 0

ATTACK OF THE FRONTEND

When there is no more hope. When
Winter is coming. When
the White Walkers prepare to break down the Wall
and JAVA can no longer stop them ...

Comes he - Javascript. Lord of untyped
data and components, he does not give
up technical tasks "to do nicely" and is not
afraid of calculations on the user side. But he comes
not alone, having 3 of his faithful friends with him:
jQuery, React.js and Node.js.
